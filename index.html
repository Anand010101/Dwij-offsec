The Reality

Most Organizations Don't Know What They're Missing

Attackers are patient, thorough, and constantly probing. A single overlooked vulnerability can be the entry point that costs everything. The question isn't if you'll be targeted — it's when, and whether you'll be ready.

You shipped new features — did your security keep up?

Every new endpoint, API, or integration is a potential attack surface. Development moves fast; vulnerabilities accumulate silently.

Automated scanners only find what they're programmed to look for

Real attackers exploit business logic, chained vulnerabilities, and misconfigurations that no automated tool can detect without human expertise.

Compliance ≠ Security

Passing an audit means you met a checklist — not that you're actually secure. Attackers don't follow compliance frameworks.

How We Work

A Clear, Structured Process — No Surprises

We believe security testing should be transparent. Here's exactly how every engagement runs, from first contact to final report.

01

Free Scoping Consultation

We start with a conversation — no obligation, no sales pitch. We listen to your environment, goals, and concerns, then recommend the right scope and approach for your situation.

02

Engagement Agreement & NDA

Everything is documented and legally protected before testing begins. You'll receive a clear scope of work and a mutual NDA so your data is always confidential.

03

Reconnaissance & Attack Surface Mapping

We map your entire attack surface — endpoints, technologies, exposed services, and entry points — using both passive and active intelligence gathering techniques.

04

Manual Testing & Exploitation

Our testers manually probe every target. We identify vulnerabilities, verify they're exploitable, and document the exact impact — including chained attacks that automated tools miss entirely.

05

Detailed Report Delivery

You receive a complete report with an executive summary, full technical findings, proof-of-concept for every vulnerability, risk ratings, and prioritized remediation steps your team can act on.

06

Debrief & Remediation Support

We walk through every finding with your team, answer questions, clarify fixes, and remain available during remediation. Once you've fixed things, we'll retest critical findings at no extra charge.

Why Dwij Offensive Security

What Makes Us Different From Every Other Option

Most Important

We Find What Others Miss

We don't use automated scanners as our primary tool. Our testers manually explore every application the way a real attacker would — including business logic, chained vulnerabilities, and edge cases. This is why manual testing finds 3–4× more critical vulnerabilities than scanner-only approaches.

Reports You Can Actually Use

Our reports are written for two audiences: executives who need the business impact picture, and developers who need to understand and fix each vulnerability. No jargon walls. No vague recommendations.

You Work With the Tester, Not a Project Manager

With larger firms you often get a salesperson who hands you off to a junior tester you'll never speak to. With us, the person who tested your system is the same person who explains the findings and helps you fix them.

Full Transparency, Always

You'll know exactly what we're testing, how we're testing it, and what we found. We don't hide findings that are inconvenient, and we don't pad reports with low-severity noise to look thorough.

Free Retest After Remediation

Finding vulnerabilities is only half the job. After you've fixed the critical and high-severity issues, we retest them at no extra cost to confirm the fixes are effective and no new issues were introduced.

Strict Confidentiality

Every engagement begins with a mutual NDA. Your systems, findings, and business information are never shared, never discussed, and handled with the same confidentiality you'd expect from a trusted partner.
