// OUR PROCESS

Testing Methodology

A structured, battle-proven approach that mirrors real adversary tactics.

Reconnaissance

OSINT gathering, attack surface mapping, and passive/active information collection about the target environment.

Vulnerability Analysis

Manual and tool-assisted identification of security weaknesses, logic flaws, and misconfigurations in the target.

Exploitation

Safe and controlled exploitation of discovered vulnerabilities to demonstrate real-world impact and severity.

Post-Exploitation

Lateral movement, privilege escalation, and persistence analysis to understand the full breach potential.

Reporting & Remediation

Detailed report with executive summary, technical findings, PoC evidence, and prioritized remediation roadmap.
